# 🚀 What’s Next (In Order)

## 1️⃣ Product Lock-In (No More Feature Creep)

Before launch you need to say:

> “These are the core features. Everything else waits.”

For your MVP relaunch, the core should be:

* Job creation
* Scheduling
* Time tracking
* Quote → Invoice generation
* Basic client details
* Team accounts (if stable)

If it does those **cleanly and reliably**, you’re ready.

Not perfect. Reliable.

---

## 2️⃣ Production Readiness (Critical)

You’ve talked a lot about competing with ServiceM8 and Tradify.
That means you must check:

### 🔐 Accounts & Payments

* Subscriptions logic works?
* Free → Paid transition works?
* Stripe live mode ready?
* Trial logic correct?

### 📱 Mobile + Web

* Responsive?
* No layout breaking?
* No obvious UI bugs?

### 📩 Email

* Invoice emails sending correctly?
* Branded correctly?
* SPF/DKIM configured?

### 💾 Data Integrity

* No duplicate jobs?
* No invoice miscalculations?
* No timer drift?

You don’t launch until this is clean.

---

## 3️⃣ Landing Page (This Is Massive)

Your landing page should now:

### Clear Headline

Not:

> “Powerful all-in-one solution for trade professionals”

But:

> “Jobs. Quotes. Invoices. Done.”

### One Clear CTA

“Start Free Trial”

### Short Demo Video (real UI)

Looped hero video builds trust instantly.

---

## 4️⃣ Relaunch Strategy (Soft First)

Don’t blast ads yet.

Start with:

### Phase 1 – Controlled Launch

* 5–10 local tradies
* Your crane contact
* Your existing testers

Watch:

* Where they hesitate
* What confuses them
* What they complain about

That feedback is gold.

---

## 5️⃣ Relaunch Content

You need:

### 🎥 1 Main Video (60 sec)

Real UI.
Clear voice.
Confidence.

### 📱 3 Short Hooks

Example:

**Hook 1**
“Still writing quotes on paper?”

**Hook 2**
“Stop chasing unpaid invoices.”

**Hook 3**
“This is how tradies should run jobs.”

Simple. Real. Clean.

---

## 6️⃣ Pricing Psychology

This is important.

At relaunch:

* Keep it simple.
* Don’t overcomplicate tiers.
* Make the Pro tier feel obvious.

Example:

Free → Solo tradie
Pro → $39–$59/month
Team → higher

Clarity > complexity.
