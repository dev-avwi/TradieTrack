🔹 1. Speed under load

🔹 2. Conflict intelligence

Top systems automatically detect:
	•	Double-booked worker
	•	Overlapping jobs
	•	Travel time impossible
	•	Skill mismatch (electrician booked for plumbing)
	•	Equipment double booking

Do you have:
	•	Skill matching?
	•	Equipment conflict alerts?
	•	Drive-time feasibility logic?

If not, that’s a gap.

🔹 3. Auto-Dispatch Intelligence

The next level systems have:
	•	“Assign nearest available worker”
	•	“Optimize route for the day”
	•	“Reshuffle jobs due to cancellation”
	•	“Show who has spare capacity”

Your map is strong.

But do you have dispatch intelligence?

That’s what separates good from elite.

🔹 4. Permission-tight dispatch control

Can:
	•	Workers change status?
	•	Workers reassign jobs?
	•	Workers edit schedule?

🔹 5. Audit-grade activity log
