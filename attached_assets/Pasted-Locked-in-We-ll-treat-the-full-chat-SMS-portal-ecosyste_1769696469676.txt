🧱 TradieTrack — Lean MVP Messaging & Workflow

🎯 MVP Goal

Allow a tradie to run a job end-to-end professionally with:
	•	Simple client communication
	•	Minimal automation
	•	No confusion
	•	No expensive infrastructure
	•	Fast development

If this works smoothly → we scale features later.

⸻

✅ What Stays in MVP (Non-Negotiable)

These are the features that actually deliver value immediately.

⸻

1️⃣ Single Chat Per Job (No Direct Messages)

Instead of:
	•	Job Chat
	•	Direct Messages
	•	Team Chat

👉 MVP has:

✅ One Chat Thread per Job

Everything related to that job lives in one place:
	•	Messages
	•	Photos
	•	Updates

No switching contexts.
No confusion.

⸻

Tradie Chat UI

Inside each job:
	•	Message input
	•	Attach photo button
	•	Send button
	•	No SMS toggle
	•	No advanced settings

Simple = fast adoption.

⸻

⸻

2️⃣ Smart Default Messaging (Portal First, SMS Only When It Matters)

MVP Logic

Situation	Delivery
Normal messages	Portal only
Quote sent	SMS auto
On My Way	SMS auto
Invoice sent	SMS auto

Tradie never decides delivery mode manually.

This:
	•	Removes cognitive load
	•	Prevents accidental SMS charges
	•	Prevents “why didn’t they see my message?” confusion

⸻

Example

Tradie types:

“I’ll bring the replacement valve.”

→ Client sees it inside portal only.

Tradie taps:

“On My Way”

→ Client receives SMS automatically with portal link.

Zero thinking required.

⸻

⸻

3️⃣ Lightweight Client Portal (Very Simple)

MVP Portal Shows:
	•	Business name
	•	Job title
	•	Status
	•	Message feed
	•	Photo upload
	•	View invoice + pay

❌ No live tracking
❌ No contact cards
❌ No worker profiles
❌ No advanced UI

Just:

Open link → See job → Message → Upload photo → Pay

Fast loading. Minimal bugs.

4️⃣ Client Photo Upload → Auto Attach to Job

This stays. It’s a huge differentiator.

When client uploads:
	•	Photos auto-save into Job Photos
	•	Labeled “Client Upload”
	•	Tradie sees instantly

This alone adds real professional value.

5️⃣ Only 3 Auto SMS in MVP

SMS is powerful but expensive and risky early.

MVP Auto SMS:

✅ Quote Sent
✅ On My Way
✅ Invoice Sent

That’s it.

No reminders.
No overdue flows.
No completion messages.

You can add later once traction exists.

⸻

⸻

6️⃣ No Live GPS Tracking (Yet)

For MVP:

Instead of:

Live moving map

Use:

Simple “On My Way” timestamp

Example client sees:

“Jake is on the way — ETA approx 15–25 mins.”

This avoids:
	•	Battery drain
	•	Permission bugs
	•	Legal issues
	•	Infra costs

You can layer live tracking later once revenue exists.
