# ⚖️ 3️⃣ TERMS & CONDITIONS YOU NEED

This is the MOST important part.

Below is what you should tell Replit to include in your legal pages.

---

## 🛡️ A. Invoice Accuracy Disclaimer

You need a clause that says:

> The user is responsible for reviewing all generated invoices before sending.
> The platform does not guarantee accuracy of time, rates, calculations, or totals.

This protects you from:

* $500 missing invoice disputes
* “The app calculated wrong” claims

---

## 🛡️ B. Time Tracking Disclaimer

Include:

> Time tracking data may be affected by device performance, GPS availability, battery life, and connectivity.
> Users are responsible for verifying recorded hours.

This protects against:

* GPS drift
* Timer bugs
* Background app restrictions

---

## 🛡️ C. GPS Liability Protection

Add:

> GPS location data may be inaccurate or unavailable due to signal interference.
> The platform is not liable for disputes arising from GPS discrepancies.

---

## 🛡️ D. Edited Time Log Clause

Include:

> Edited time entries will be logged within the system.
> Users acknowledge that manual adjustments override original tracked data.

This prevents:

* Fraud claims
* “The system changed my time” accusations

---

## 🛡️ E. No Financial Escrow Clause

VERY IMPORTANT.

If you're NOT handling client payments directly:

> The platform does not act as an escrow service or financial intermediary.
> Users are responsible for handling payments independently.

If you integrate Stripe later:
You must state:

> Payment processing is handled by third-party providers (e.g., Stripe).
> The platform does not store credit card information.

---

## 🛡️ F. Limitation of Liability

Add:

> The platform’s liability is limited to the subscription fee paid within the last 30 days.

This caps your legal exposure.

---

## 🛡️ G. Data Loss Clause

> The platform is not responsible for data loss due to user device failure or force majeure events.

---

## 🛡️ H. Subscription Terms

Include:

* Monthly billing cycle
* Cancellation policy
* No refunds after billing period begins
* Trial period terms (if offered)

---

## 🛡️ I. Intellectual Property

> All platform software, branding, and design remain the property of [Your Company Name].
