Best option: **one tracking link per job**, and it can show **one “primary” live tracker** by default, with a toggle to view **other assigned workers** (if you want). That’s the cleanest for clients and the least confusing.

### Why this is best

* **Client simplicity:** one link, one place. No “which link do I click?”
* **Less spam:** you don’t send multiple SMS threads.
* **Scales to big teams:** 3–10 workers on a job is possible.
* **More control:** you can decide how much to reveal (privacy + subcontractors).
