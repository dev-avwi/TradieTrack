MASTER UI REFINEMENT DIRECTIVE

Refactor this screen to follow JobRunner Premium Mobile Design System:

	6.	Increase contrast between sections using subtle background tint blocks.
	7.	Introduce micro-depth:

	•	Slight card elevation
	8.	Reduce visual repetition.

	9.	Add subtle motion affordances (press feedback, hover scaling).
