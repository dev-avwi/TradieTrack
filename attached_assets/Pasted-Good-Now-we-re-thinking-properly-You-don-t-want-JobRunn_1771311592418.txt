🧱 CORE FOUNDATION (Must Be Rock Solid)

Before adding features, this must be bulletproof:

1️⃣ Single Source of Truth

Every job must feed:
	•	Timeline (append-only event log)
	•	Financial engine
	•	Client portal
	•	Dispatch map
	•	Reporting dashboard

No duplicate states.
No silent overwrites.
Everything logs.

⸻

2️⃣ Role Architecture (Clean + Scalable)

Roles:

Owner
	•	Full access
	•	Financial reports
	•	Staff rates
	•	Profit margins
	•	Automation rules

Manager / Dispatcher
	•	Assign jobs
	•	Manage crew
	•	View reports (optional)
	•	Approve timesheets

Worker
	•	Assigned jobs only
	•	Clock in/out
	•	Upload photos/materials
	•	Limited financial visibility

Subcontractor
	•	Assignment scoped
	•	Must accept job
	•	Limited client visibility
	•	Optional web-only mode

Office Admin
	•	Quotes
	•	Invoices
	•	Client communication
	•	No time tracking

This makes it scalable.
