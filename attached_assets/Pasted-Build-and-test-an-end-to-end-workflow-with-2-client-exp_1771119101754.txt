Build and test an end-to-end workflow with 2 client experiences:

(1) Job Tracking Link (no login): Uber-style live travel map + ETA + updates (read-only).
(2) Client Portal (verified): Client can view all their jobs/quotes/invoices and pay invoices after OTP verification.

SECURITY REQUIREMENT:
- Tracking Link is token-based, scoped to 1 job + 1 assignment, read-only, no payment actions.
- Client Portal requires OTP verification (SMS or email code) and then uses a secure session.

DATA MODELS REQUIRED (minimum):
- Client {id, name, phone, email}
- Job {id, company_id, client_id, address, lat, lng, status}
- Quote {id, job_id, amount, pdf_url, status}
- Invoice {id, job_id, amount_due, pdf_url, status, payment_link_url}
- JobAssignment {id, job_id, worker_user_id, status, rate_override, show_worker_phone_to_client}
- PortalToken {token, job_id, assignment_id, expires_at, revoked_at}
- ClientSession {client_id, session_token, expires_at}
- OTPChallenge {client_id, channel, code_hash, expires_at, attempts, used_at}

OTP FLOW REQUIREMENTS (Client Portal):
1) Client clicks “Open Client Portal” from Tracking Link page.
2) They enter phone/email (pre-filled if possible from job data).
3) Send OTP via SMS/email (6-digit).
4) Client enters OTP.
5) If correct, create ClientSession token (httpOnly cookie or secure token) and redirect into Client Portal dashboard.
6) Client Portal dashboard shows:
   - Active jobs
   - Past jobs
   - Quotes
   - Invoices
   - Payment buttons for unpaid invoices
7) Client can open/download quote/invoice PDFs.
8) Client can click “Pay Now” -> redirect to Stripe hosted payment link OR in-app checkout.
9) After payment webhook confirms success, mark invoice PAID and update portal automatically.

ANTI-FRAUD / SAFETY:
- OTP expires in 10 minutes
- Max 5 attempts
- Rate limit OTP sends (e.g., 3 per hour)
- Client Portal only shows jobs/invoices for that client_id
- Tracking Link tokens are long, random, and expire 7 days after job completion
- Tracking Link cannot expose payment actions or sensitive account settings

UBER-STYLE TRACKING LINK REQUIREMENTS:
- Tracking page route: /p/{token}
- Shows business name/logo, owner/dispatch phone, job status timeline.
- Live map + ETA ONLY while assignment status = EN_ROUTE.
- Live location updates every 30–60 seconds; show “last updated” and accuracy.
- Stop tracking when ARRIVED confirmed or timeout (e.g. 120 mins).
- Before/after photos and documents can be viewed on tracking page (read-only).
- Include a clear button: “Open Client Portal (view invoices & pay)” -> triggers OTP flow.

WORKER “ON MY WAY” SMS WORKFLOW (MUST USE ASSIGNMENT CONTEXT):
- Worker taps On My Way on an assigned job.
- Backend validates worker owns that assignment_id.
- Generate/refresh PortalToken for that assignment.
- Compute ETA.
- Send client SMS:
  "JobRunner — {BusinessName}: {WorkerName} is on the way. ETA {ETA}. Track live: {TrackingLink}. Need help? Call {OwnerPhone}. To view invoices & pay, open portal from the link."
