How ServiceM8 Does It (Smart)
They DON'T do true two-way SMS. They cheat:

SMS SENT TO CLIENT:
"Hi Sarah, Mike is on his way!
Reply here: servicem8.com/reply/abc123"
         ↓
CLIENT CLICKS LINK
         ↓
OPENS WEB CHAT PORTAL (not SMS reply)
         ↓
CLIENT TYPES REPLY + ATTACHES PHOTOS
         ↓
SHOWS UP IN TRADIE'S APP
The trick: Client doesn't text back. They click a link and reply via web. This avoids all the complex Twilio two-way routing.

How Tradify Does It (Simpler)
AUTOMATED SMS (quotes, invoices, reminders):
   → Sent through Tradify's system
   → No reply tracking
"ON MY WAY" MESSAGES:
   → Sent from tradie's PERSONAL PHONE
   → Not tracked in app
   → Free for Tradify (tradie pays their own phone bill)
Translation: Tradify basically opens your phone's SMS app with a pre-filled message. Not in-app.
