## 1️⃣ OLD WORKFLOW — REAL WORLD USE (Current App)

### Scenario

Mike (electrician) finishes a job for Sarah.

### What actually happens

1. Mike finishes the job

2. He opens TradieTrack

3. Creates an invoice

4. Taps **Send Invoice**

5. Sarah receives an **email**:

   * PDF attached
   * Stripe “Pay Now” link
   * QR code

6. Sarah:

   * Might see it immediately
   * Might see it hours later
   * Might miss it completely

7. If she pays → done

8. If she doesn’t see it → Mike waits or has to chase

### That’s it.

No feedback loop.
No updates.
No proof of work.
No recovery if email is lost.

---

### OLD WORKFLOW — WHY IT EXISTS

This is a **classic MVP** flow:

* Dead simple
* Cheap (email only)
* Very little to break
* Easy Apple approval

And that’s **fine** for launch.

## 2️⃣ NEW WORKFLOW — REAL WORLD USE (Phase 2)

Same tradie. Same customer.

### Step-by-step reality

### A) Before the job

Sarah finds **Mike’s website** and fills out *Get a Quote*.

* Lead appears in TradieTrack automatically
* Mike calls Sarah (human interaction still happens)
* Mike sends a quote through TradieTrack

📩 **SMS to Sarah**

> “Mike’s Electrical: Your quote is ready. View & accept: [portal link]”

Sarah taps, reviews, accepts. No login.

---

### B) Day of the job

Mike taps **On My Way**.

📩 **SMS**

> “Mike’s Electrical: Mike is on the way. ETA ~20 mins.”

Sarah feels informed → trust increases.

---

### C) Job complete

Mike uploads photos, marks job complete.

📩 **SMS**

> “Job complete! View photos and details: [portal link]”

Sarah:

* Sees proof of work
* Feels confident she’s paying for real value

---

### D) Invoice + payment

Mike sends invoice.

📩 **SMS**

> “Invoice ready. Pay online: [portal link]”

Sarah pays immediately (SMS open rate is insane).

📩 **SMS**

> “Thanks! Payment of $385 received.”

---

### E) Lost link scenario (THIS is the big upgrade)

A month later Sarah needs the receipt.

She:

* Goes to `tradietrack.com.au/portal`
* Enters phone number
* Gets **OTP code**
* Sees her invoices
* Downloads receipt

No emails.
No “can you resend?”
No friction.

## 4️⃣ IS THE NEW DESIGN ACTUALLY SECURE ENOUGH?

### Short answer: **Yes — for this use case.**

You are **not** protecting bank accounts or identity documents.
You are protecting:

* invoices
* job photos
* payment links

Using:

* Magic links **plus**
* OTP fallback

This is **industry standard**.

Even big players like Jobber and ServiceM8 rely on:

* token links
* phone/email verification
* NOT full user accounts for clients

### Security rating:

* Old system: **7/10** (email can be forwarded, inbox hacked)
* New system: **8–8.5/10** (OTP + expiring tokens)

That’s an upgrade.
