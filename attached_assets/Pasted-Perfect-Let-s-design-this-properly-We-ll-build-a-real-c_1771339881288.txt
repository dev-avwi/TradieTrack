This example assumes:

* Business: Elite Crane Hire
* Owner: Sarah
* Operator: Tom
* Dogman/Rigger: Josh
* Subcontracted transport truck: Metro Heavy Haul
* Equipment: Liebherr LTM 1060 (60T crane)
* Client: BuildCorp Pty Ltd

---

# 🏗 FULL CRANE JOB WORKFLOW (JobRunner)

---

# 1️⃣ Client Calls → Job Created

### Step 1: Owner Creates Job

Owner (Sarah) opens JobRunner → New Job:

```
Client: BuildCorp Pty Ltd
Site: 25 Wharf Street, Brisbane
Job Type: Steel lift
Date: 18 March
Start Time: 7:00 AM
Estimated Duration: 6 hours
```

Adds:

* Estimated crane size required
* Notes: “Lift 4 steel beams to Level 3”
* Uploads lift plan PDF

Presses: Save

---

# 2️⃣ Assign Equipment + Crew

Inside Job:

### Equipment Assignment

Select:

* Liebherr LTM 1060
* Hourly Rate: $350/hr
* Travel Fee: $450

### Crew Assignment

* Tom (Operator) – $110/hr
* Josh (Dogman) – $90/hr
* Metro Heavy Haul (Subcontractor truck)

System checks:

✅ Is crane already booked?
❌ If yes → show conflict warning.

---

# 3️⃣ Dispatch Confirmation

Press: Dispatch Job

What happens automatically:

### To Workers

Tom receives:

> You have been assigned to Job #482 – BuildCorp – 7:00 AM tomorrow.

With:

* Map
* Job notes
* Contact details

Josh receives same.

### To Subcontractor (No App Required)

Metro Heavy Haul receives SMS:

> You’ve been assigned to transport Liebherr LTM 1060 for Job #482.
> View details: secure link (OTP protected)

---

# 4️⃣ Client Confirmation SMS

Client receives:

> Elite Crane Hire has scheduled your lift for 18 March at 7:00 AM.
> View job details & track crew: secure link

This link is Uber-style tracking.

---

# 5️⃣ Morning of Job – “On My Way”

Tom opens JobRunner → clicks “On My Way”.

System does:

* Starts location tracking
* Calculates ETA
* Updates client portal

Client now sees:

🗺 Map:

* Tom (Operator)
* Josh (Rigger)
* Crane icon
* ETA: 22 mins

Status:

> Operator en route

---

# 6️⃣ Geofence Auto Clock-In

When Tom enters site radius (100m):

System:

* Auto clock-in
* Status updates to: “On Site”
* Client sees: “Crew has arrived”

No manual time entry required.

---

# 7️⃣ During Job

Tom logs:

* 6.5 hours worked
* Adds:

  * Extra lifting spreader attachment
  * 2 extra lift cycles
* Uploads site photos
* Uploads signed SWMS

Subcontractor uploads:

* Transport docket
* Arrival photo

All synced to job timeline.

---

# 8️⃣ Real-Time Owner Dashboard

Owner sees:

```
LIVE JOBS:
#482 – On Site
Crane: Active
Operator: 4.3 hours logged
Dogman: 4.3 hours logged
Subcontractor: Completed transport
```

No calls needed.

---

# 9️⃣ Job Completion

Tom presses: Complete Job.

System:

* Stops timer
* Calculates total hours
* Applies:

  * Crane rate
  * Operator rate
  * Dogman rate
  * Travel
  * Extras
* Generates invoice draft

---

# 🔟 Automatic Invoice Breakdown

Invoice shows:

```
Liebherr LTM 1060 – 6.5 hrs @ $350/hr = $2,275
Operator (Tom) – 6.5 hrs @ $110/hr = $715
Dogman (Josh) – 6.5 hrs @ $90/hr = $585
Travel Fee = $450
Spreader Attachment = $180
Total: $4,205 + GST
```

Time logs attached.

Audit trail included.

---

# 1️⃣1️⃣ Client Portal View

Client clicks link.

They see:

* Job timeline
* Arrival time
* Completion time
* Photos
* Documents
* Invoice
* Pay Now button

They can’t dispute:
Everything is visible.

# 1️⃣2️⃣ Payment

System:

* Marks Paid

Owner sees:

> Job #482 Paid – $4,205
