## Verdict: READY TO PUBLISH

**TradieTrack is ready for launch.** It offers a complete job management solution that:

1. **Matches** Tradify/ServiceM8 on core functionality
2. **Exceeds** competitors on AI features, team tracking, and modern UI
3. **Has room to grow** with customer portal and forms library

### Recommended Launch Strategy:

1. **Soft Launch** - Invite 5-10 Cairns tradies for real-world testing
2. **Gather Feedback** - Focus on daily workflow reliability
3. **Iterate** - Fix any edge cases found in production
4. **Full Launch** - Public release after 2-4 weeks of beta feedback
